# VET Practice & Facilitation


> "Effective design is born in the classroom."

While I specialize in Instructional Design, my foundation is built on years of direct experience facilitating adult learning in the **Vocational Education and Training (VET)** sector. I am currently completing my **Cert IV in Training and Assessment (TAE40122)** to further align my practice with national standards and ASQA compliance.

---

## My Approach: The Practitioner’s Edge
Because I teach the content I design, I bring a unique "practitioner’s lens" to every project. This ensures that the resources I create are:
* **Trainable:** Logistically sound for facilitators to use in real-time.
* **Compliant:** Mapped accurately to Units of Competency (UoC).
* **Engaging:** Built using adult learning principles that respect the learner's prior experience.
